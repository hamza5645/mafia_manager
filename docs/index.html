Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Mafia Society ("we", "our", or "the app") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Solo Mode

When using Solo Mode, all game data (player names, roles, game history) is stored locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any personal information. No account is required.

Multiplayer Mode

When using Multiplayer Mode, you may choose to create an account. If you do, we collect:

  • Email address — Used for account authentication
  • Display name — Shown to other players in game sessions
  • Game session data — Room codes, player actions, and game state for real-time synchronization

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate your account
  • Enable multiplayer game sessions
  • Synchronize game state between players in real-time

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

Our multiplayer functionality is powered by Supabase, which provides authentication and real-time database services. Supabase processes data in accordance with their Privacy Policy.

Data Storage and Security

  • Solo Mode data is stored only on your device and never leaves it.
  • Multiplayer data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption in transit and at rest.

Data Retention

Game session data is retained only for the duration of active game sessions. Completed game sessions may be deleted automatically. Account information is retained until you request deletion.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Use Solo Mode without creating an account

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for party game entertainment and may be used by players of all ages under adult supervision.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
